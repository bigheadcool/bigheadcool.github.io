An interaction designer and creative technologist focusing on experience design, interactive installation, and creative coding.

As a versatile person adapted at learning new things and integrating different skills, I started as an electrical engineering student from National Taiwan University, interesting in making and tinkering with electronics. I decided to came to San Francisco in 2012 to study at California College of the Arts and became an interaction designer, exploring user experience design and the creativity in new technologies.

My passion is to explore the interaction and the emotional connections between people and technology, combining physical and digital experiences to reimagine our everyday lives.
